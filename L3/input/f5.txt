<h2><span id="dnn_ctr723_dnnTITLE_titleLabel" class="Head">Submission of papers</span>


</h2>

<div id="Agg723_Tab"><ul class="jQueryUI_Tabs_TabStrip"><li><a href="#Agg723_1">Submissions Information</a></li>

<div id="Agg723_1" class="jQueryUI_Tabs_TabPage"><p><strong>EHB 2011, EHB 2013,&nbsp;<strong>EHB 2015,&nbsp;</strong>EHB 2017,&nbsp;<strong>EHB 2019,&nbsp;EHB 2020&nbsp;and <span style="color: #c00000;">EHB 2021</span>&nbsp;</strong>were already accepted and indexed in ISI Conference Proceedings Citation Index.</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The review process requires full paper submission. <br />
<br />
All submissions will be written in English (USA), with a <strong><em>maximum paper length of 4 pages (and minimum of 3.5 pages) for regular papers</em></strong>&nbsp;and <strong><em>6 pages for invited papers</em></strong>. Yet, if authors want to write their paper with 6 pages in length, <strong><span style="color: #c00000;">they have to pay an extra fee of 100 euros</span></strong>.&nbsp;</p>
<strong>Only PDF files&nbsp;</strong>are accepted for the review process but final paper (accepted)&nbsp;<strong><span style="color: #c00000;">must also be sent in Word format </span></strong>to <strong>paper@ehbconference.ro
</strong>
<p>The official language of EHB 2023 is&nbsp;<strong>English</strong>.</p>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="text-align: left; color: #000000;"><em>A first camera ready manuscript</em> </span><span style="text-align: left;">describing work not previously published have to be electronically submitted in PDF file (maximum 2 MB)</span><span style="text-align: left; color: #000000;"> through the easyChair website</span><span style="text-align: left; color: #c00000;">,&nbsp;</span><a href="https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=ehbweb2023" target="_blank" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: #c00000;">https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=ehbweb2023</span></a><span style="text-align: left; color: #c00000;">, <strong>beginning with 15th June 2023 and&nbsp;till</strong>&nbsp;</span><strong style="text-align: left;"><span style="text-decoration: line-through;">1st OCTOBER 2023</span></strong><span style="text-align: left;"><strong><span style="color: #c00000;">&nbsp;<span style="text-decoration: underline;">12th OCTOBER 2023</span> WITHOUT AUTHOR&rsquo;S NAME AND AFFILIATION !</span></strong></span><br />
</div>
<p>In order to submit papers for the <strong>International E-health and Bioengineering Conference &ndash; EHB 2023</strong>&nbsp;you have to create an EasyChair account. <br />
<a href="/Portals/0/Account_Tutorial_EasyChair_2023.pdf" target="_blank" style="text-decoration-line: underline;">Download Create Account Tutorial EasyChair</a><br />
<br />
</p>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><strong><span style="color: #c00000;">Authors of the best papers with specific topics will be invited to complete their articles with new outcomes, in order to be submitted to Sensors or Biosensors journals:&nbsp;</span></strong><br />
<h3><strong>
<div style="text-align: left;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;<a href="https://www.mdpi.com/journal/biosensors/special_issues/E641IOL248" target="_blank"><img alt="" src="/portals/0/Bioensors_banner400x229.png" /></a><br />
</div>
<br />
<a href="/Portals/0/Sensors_flyer.pdf" target="_blank">Download SENSORS Flyer</a></strong><a href="/Portals/0/Biosensors_flyer.pdf" target="_blank"><br />
<br />
</a><a href="https://www.mdpi.com/journal/sensors/special_issues/1I7IW852MS" target="_blank">https://www.mdpi.com/journal/sensors/special_issues/1I7IW852MS<br />
</a><a href="https://www.mdpi.com/journal/biosensors/special_issues/E641IOL248" target="_blank">https://www.mdpi.com/journal/biosensors/special_issues/E641IOL248</a></h3>
<strong><span style="color: #ff0000;">Of course, other new papers are also welcome for these journals.</span></strong>
<strong><strong><em><br />
</em></strong></strong></div></div><div id="Agg723_2" class="jQueryUI_Tabs_TabPage"><p style="text-align: justify;">The camera ready manuscript in A4 format (21 x 29,7 cm) should have <em><strong>4 pages</strong>&nbsp;(<strong style="color: #222222; font-family: &quot;Lucida Grande&quot;, &quot;Lucida Sans&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.2px; text-align: justify; background-color: #fcfdfd;"><em>and minimum of 3.5 pages)&nbsp;</em></strong></em>in length and <strong><em>invited papers may have 4 or 6 pages</em></strong> (PDF file format only, maximum 2 MB)<em>,</em> and must conform to double column format. Submissions exceeding 6 pages will be rejected. <br />
<br />
<em><strong>Do NOT insert any headers, footers, or page numbers</strong></em>, and <strong><em>do NOT apply security settings</em></strong> to your PDF file. Templates for papers are available below in DOC format, without and with authors&rsquo; names, respectively. Violations of the paper template or the paper length (4 pages) will not be accepted.</p>
